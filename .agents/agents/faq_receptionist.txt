    Orders typically ship from our warehouse within 36-48 business hours if the item is in stock. For large or heavy shipments, the trucking carrier will contact you to schedule a delivery time.
  </faq>

Please inspect packages upon delivery and report visible damage or wrong/missing items to customerservice@arielbath.com or (800) 827-5950.
  </faq>
